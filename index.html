About US

Safe Environment and Health Organization (SEHO) was registered (Registration # OP.218/051/2008/0313/5315) as a national Non Governmental Organization in 2008. SEHO currently operates in three counties i.e. Kisumu, Homa Bay and Migori. It works through partnerships with local implementing partners; civil society organizations (CSOs), Educationists/community mentors and the County Government. The organization seeks to increase access to education, improved livelihood and good health in addition to sound environmental practices. SEHO’s main target populations include; youths in and out of school, Women groups and selected Friends of Environment to propagate for safe environment. SEHO seeks to demonstrate an integrated community model that strives to draw from the local resources to ensure sustainability with an in-built community ownership and participation at all levels. It operates on the principle that the solid foundation to good health and safe environment is vested in the people who are adequately empowered to effectively participate on issues that affect their own lives with regard to health and environment.
